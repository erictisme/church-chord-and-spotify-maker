## For Music Leaders (Non-Technical)

### How to Use (3 Easy Steps):

#### Step 1: Open the Song Selector
- **Visit the website:** https://erictisme.github.io/church-chord-and-spotify-maker/
- **Bookmark it** for easy access!
- OR **Download the file:** [index.html](./index.html) and double-click to open offline

#### Step 2: Fill in Service Details
1. Enter the **Service Date**
2. Enter the **Scripture Passage** (e.g., "Deuteronomy 18:15-19, Acts 7:35-60")
3. Enter the **Theme** (e.g., "Jesus fulfils God's law")

#### Step 3: Select Songs
1. **Search** for songs by typing keywords (song title, artist name, or themes)
2. **Click on theme tags** (worship, praise, cross, etc.) to filter songs
3. **Click on songs** to select them (you'll see them in the "Selected Songs" list)
4. Select 4-5 songs for your service

#### Step 4: Generate Document
1. Click **"Generate Word Document"** button at the bottom
2. A `.docx` file will download automatically (e.g., `FMH-Worship-2025-11-17.docx`)
3. **Open the file** in:
   - **Google Docs** (upload to Google Drive, then open)
   - **Microsoft Word** (OneDrive or desktop)

### Troubleshooting:
**Q: The page says "Loading songs..." forever**
- Check your internet connection
- Refresh the page (F5 or Cmd+R)

**Q: I can't find a specific song**
- Try searching by artist name or theme instead of title
- Contact Eric if the song should be in the library

**Q: The generated document looks weird**
- Make sure you open it in Word or Google Docs (not a text editor)
- The formatting is optimized for Arial font

**Q: I need help!**
